# Client Segmentation Project

⚠️ All data used in this project is entirely synthetic and generated for demonstration purposes only.

**Cluster interpretation**

   *Cluster 0 – New Steady Clients*
- Moderate sales and purchase frequency.
- Low product diversity and short relationship (4 years).
- Low discount rate, average payment delay (~32 days).
Profile: Newly acquired customers with consistent but limited activity.

    *Cluster 1 – Mature and Regular Clients*
- Older customers (10+ years) with stable purchasing behavior.
- Slightly lower sales and longer payment delays (40 days).
- Decent diversity, moderate loyalty.
Profile: Long-term clients with steady but slowing activity.

    *Cluster 2 – High-Value Loyal Performers*
- Highest purchase frequency (61 per year).
- Fast payments (≈14 days), excellent loyalty score (173).
- Mid-level diversity, good sales volume.
Profile: Key strategic accounts, consistent, loyal, and reliable.

    *Cluster 3 – Price-Sensitive Opportunists*
- Highest annual sales but high discount rate (0.19).
- Fewer transactions per year, moderate loyalty.
- Medium product diversity, average payment time.
Profile: High spenders motivated by promotions and discounts.

   *Cluster 4 – Diverse, Short-Term Clients*
- High product diversity (≈15 SKUs), average frequency and volume.
- Very short relationship (≈4 years).
- Moderate loyalty, normal payment behavior.
Profile: Newer clients exploring multiple products.
